Code of Conduct

It starts with me

Be at best to everyone

As a part of the community I will be professional and excellent at all times to every members of the community regardless of gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disabilities, neurodiversity, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, race, age & religion. I will treat everyone with respect. I acknowledge that everyone deserves to learn and share knowledge. With this, I will not discriminate or harass anyone and that I am responsible for all of my actions.

Speak up when I see or hear something

I know that harassment and discrimination is not tolerated, hence I must report any untoward incidents if ever I or someone is being disrespected. I know that by speaking up and letting authorities handle the situation, I can help myself, others and the community as a whole to maintain a safe space for everyone to express ideas and knowledge for everyone's gain.

Participate and try to give back

I will try my best to participate in events that I can be part of. I understand that community is built on everyone's effort hence I will try to give back to the community as much as I could.
